Currently, most lottery programs work in the traditional way: users can not know exactly how much prizes and how they are used. All information is from the organizers, so it is difficult to prevent embezzlement

Blockchain Lotto solved this problem by applying decentralized technology and Smart Contract.

Step 1

The player pays a deposit as participation fee (0.0005 ETH). This amount will be kept at Smart Contract. No one can interfere with this amount, including the organizers. You can see its definition at Here

Step 2

Smart Contract will randomly award prizes (details of awards see below)

Step 3

The player receives the prize anonymously, ensuring his / her privacy

Award:

The program will start weekly (if the prize is greater than 0.05 ETH)

  • 50% of the prize will be sent to the winner (random)
  • 35% of the prize will be sent to 15% of players (random)
  • 12% will be used for advertising and affiliate marketing purposes
  • 3% for developers
